Privacy Policy

This tool analyzes suspicious messages and links. Do not paste OTPs, passwords, bank PINs, full card numbers, passport numbers, NID numbers, or other sensitive personal data.

What is processed

In Cloudflare Pages Functions mode, the pasted message may be processed server-side to extract and analyze URLs. This clean starter version does not save scan history in a database.

Third-party APIs

If you add Google Safe Browsing or VirusTotal API keys, URLs may be sent to those services for reputation checks. Review their policies before using them on a public website.

Ads

If you add AdSense or another ad network, that network may use cookies or tracking technologies. You are responsible for following the ad network policies.

Disclaimer

This tool gives a risk estimate, not a guarantee. If a message is related to banking, delivery, government, jobs, or payments, verify manually through the official app or website.
